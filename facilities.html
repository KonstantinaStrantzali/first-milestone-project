ThermalSpa

We are more than a simple spa center

Private treatment room

Explore our whole range of spa experience rooms — uplifting aromas, soothing steam and calming music make the perfect sensual and relaxing environment. Our private treatment rooms are equipped with electric couchs that enhances comfort while experiencing your favourite treatment. Featuring a high-quality frame and hard-wearing cushioning, all message tables include electronically adjusted positions for additional relaxation.

Indoors Pool

Our pool is specially designed with anti-slip flooring and the swim area allows for a variety of different activities. You can swim against the swim current which allows you to adjust to your own preferred level.Control your workout intensity, movement and speed with the hydropool, which specifically matches your needs, thus creating the prefect training environment.The hydropool has a Sonos Audio Surround System so you can listen to your favourite music while relaxing or training.

Lounge Caffe

Refuel your body and soul within the serenity of Thermal Spa in our beautiful lounge cafe, which offers a selection of healthy and nourishing snacks and dishes. You can choose from a delicious range of healthy meals, designed in collaboration with nutritionists or relax with a herbal tea, cold pressed juices and a protein shake.
