About Us


A warm Welcome

EvimeriaSpa Leisure Centre, located in Putney is one of the most distinctive environments in which to restore mind, body and spirit. Owning its name to Greek word "ευημερία" EvimeriaSpa promises to bring prosperity to the costumers' life while experiencing our luxury and unique services. With more than 100 years of operation, we work hard to create a friendly day spa environment, that includes a range of modern facilities and provides affordable packages delivered by friendly, trained professionals. Our therapists harness the power of nature and science to create ground-breaking formulations that genuinely transform your skin.
